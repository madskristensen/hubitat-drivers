**Key architectural decisions:**
- `initialize()` calls `runIn(1, "openSocket")` to establish persistent connection that never closes.
- `sendHeartbeat()` self-reschedules via `runIn(HEARTBEAT_INTERVAL_SECONDS, "sendHeartbeat")` (10 s interval).
- `scheduleReconnect()` with exponential backoff; `socketStatus()` suppresses spurious reconnects within 3 s of intentional close.
- Heartbeat frame (cmd 9) must have **zero-byte payload** — special case in `encryptTuyaPayload()` to skip AES-PKCS5 padding.
- `pumpQueue` bails early if `state.socketOpen != true`; queued writes drain after reconnect via `retryPendingRequests()`.
- Push frame ambiguity accepted (cannot distinguish "response to write" from "push from remote" at protocol level); both routed through `applyDps()` for consistent state handling.
