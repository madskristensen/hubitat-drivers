## Rationale

**Root Cause:** v0.4.2's alpha-byte fix used `(0xFF << 24)` which in Groovy signed-int arithmetic produces `-16777216` (negative). When OR'd with RGB bits, result is negative — fails Gemstone API's `>= 0` validation.

**Fix:** Use `0xFFL << 24` to force long arithmetic. Result is positive Long in [4278190080, 4294967295] — within API's accepted range.
